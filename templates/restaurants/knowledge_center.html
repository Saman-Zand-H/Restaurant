Menu

This section is all about the foods provided by your restaurant. Before we jump into the details about this section, you‚Äôll need to be introduced to some terms.

Cuisine: is the most fundamental categorization for foods. For instance, if your restaurant provides different foods like Steaks, Vegetarian Food, Kebabs, Pizzas, etc. these categorizations are considered to be different cuisines.

Items: This categorization is a little more specific and are subsets of cuisines. For instance, Your steaks may include Beef steaks, or your Vegetarian Foods may include Cesar Salad, etc. This categorization is called items here.

Item Variations: Lastly, the most specific categorization is item variation here. For instance, you may have your beef steak available in two sizes, say medium and large beef steak. This specific categorization is an item variation.

Now that you now what each of these words are supposed to mean, we can get back to our guide.
